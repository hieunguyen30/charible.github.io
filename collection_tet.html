About Tet Collection

Time is flying by rapidly, and the year of the buffalo is slowly coming to an end. To start this new and exciting year off with a bang, Charible would like to announce that our first collection of NFTs has been uploaded onto Openseas after months of hard work! Our first collection is themed around the image of the tiger-this year's spirit animal. Often dubbed the embodiment of passion and ambition, the tigers, we hope, will signify a promising start to our project and astounding future success. Our Charible tigers will be presented in colorful outfits of Tet and vibrant masks accompanying these attires to promote safety precautions in this holiday season.

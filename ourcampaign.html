Our Campaign

Overview

Cán Tỷ kindergarten is a small school located in Cán Tỷ village, inside Quản Bạ town in Hà Giang. The school is struggling to maintain itself under difficult conditions in the remote areas of Hà Giang. Unlike the freshening weather of the capital, Hà Giang constantly has to suffer from freezing cold weather that would inscribe on the mind of any visitors. Extreme weather combined with the ongoing pandemic has made maintaining the school more back-breaking than ever. With the premise “Giving is receiving,” Charible wants to put in some effort and dedicate the achievement of our newborn model to improve the infrastructure as well as the teaching quality of this school. Charible’s first goal is to raise 15 million VND through our paintings and trading of our NFTs on Opensea. The money raised will be used to supply the school with the classroom stationeries and equipment needed for a better education.
